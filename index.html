About Remy

Remy is super cool. He's a rat that wants to be a cook. Quite admirable if you ask me. 10/10 would let him cook me a meal.

Remy and a carrot dancing

Remy's Characteristics

  1. Bluish-gray rat
  2. Simply adores food and its quality
  3. Saves rat friends from dying from toxic food
  4. Not your run of the mill rat
